Housing and living standards

Government departments and local authorities collect and report information about ethnicity in the following areas.

Home ownership

  • Financial help from family to buy a home
  • Home ownership

Social housing

  • Renting from a local authority or housing association (‘social housing’)

Private renting

  • Renting from a private landlord

Living standards

  • Fuel poverty
  • Non-decent homes
  • Overcrowded households

Transport

  • Car or van ownership
